Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Common Men's Health Queries

What treatments are available for erectile dysfunction?

We offer oral medications, penile injections, shockwave therapy, vacuum devices, and advanced surgical implants—tailored to each individual.

Is my treatment confidential?

Yes. All consultations and medical records are strictly confidential and handled with professional discretion.

How is premature ejaculation treated?

We use medications, counseling, and behavioral therapies to help delay ejaculation and improve confidence and control.

Can penile curvature (Peyronie’s disease) be corrected?

Yes. Non-surgical treatments and surgical correction options are available depending on the curvature severity and symptoms.

Do you treat male infertility conditions?

Yes. We offer comprehensive evaluation and treatment for male infertility including sperm analysis, hormone testing, and surgical solutions.

When should I consult a specialist?

If you experience difficulty in erection, early ejaculation, infertility, or penile discomfort, it’s best to consult early for effective treatment.
